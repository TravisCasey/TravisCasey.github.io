Background & Interests

Some of my more prominent passions include physical fitness, dense mathematics texts, board games, and a good cup of coffee in the morning. I chose to further these interests at the University of Oklahoma, where I earned bachelor's degrees in mathematics and physics. My senior year I worked as a research assistant with the OU Center for Quantum Research and Technology; feel free to check out my project here.

I stuck around at OU to pursue a master's degree in applied mathematics and to work as a graduate teaching assistant. My favorite topics are applications of high-level mathematics to real-world problems
